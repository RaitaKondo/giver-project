Safe by Design

Traditional social media is built on dopamine loops and vanity metrics. We've removed them all to protect the integrity of your practice.

  • close

    No Likes or Hearts

    Giving isn't about approval. We removed popularity scores to keep the focus on the act.

  • close

    No Public Comments

    Zero pressure to perform. Reflection is internal, and browsing is a quiet, meditative experience.

  • close

    No Vanity Metrics

    We don't display follower counts or streaks. Your journey is yours alone to track.
